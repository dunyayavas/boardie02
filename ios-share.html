Sharing to Boardie on iOS

iOS has limited support for PWA share targets. Here are alternative ways to share content to Boardie.

Method 1: Copy & Paste

The simplest method is to copy the link from a social media app and paste it into Boardie:

  1. Find the post you want to save in any social media app
  2. Copy the link (usually via the share button â†’ Copy Link)
  3. Open Boardie from your home screen
  4. Tap "Add Link" and paste the URL
  5. Add any tags and save

Method 2: Create an iOS Shortcut

You can create an iOS Shortcut to make sharing easier:

  1. Open the Shortcuts app on your iOS device
  2. Tap the + button to create a new shortcut
  3. Add the "Get URLs from Input" action
  4. Add the "Open URLs" action
  5. Set the URL to: YOUR_BOARDIE_URL/?shared_url=[Shortcut Input]
  6. Rename the shortcut to "Share to Boardie"
  7. Tap "Done" to save

Now you can share content by:

  1. Tapping the share button in any app
  2. Selecting "Shortcuts" from the share sheet
  3. Tapping your "Share to Boardie" shortcut

Note: Replace YOUR_BOARDIE_URL with your actual Boardie URL, such as https://dunyayavas.github.io/boardie02

Method 3: Use the Share URL

You can manually construct a share URL:

  1. Copy a link from any social media platform
  2. Open this URL in your browser: YOUR_BOARDIE_URL/?shared_url=PASTE_LINK_HERE
  3. The link will be automatically processed by Boardie

Note: These workarounds are necessary because iOS doesn't fully support the Web Share Target API that works on Android devices. We're monitoring iOS updates and will improve this experience when Apple adds better support.
